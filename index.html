Buy One Media LLC is the independent studio of Sean Sandoval — former DJ and music industry insider, Web3 gamer, and self-taught builder. The portfolio spans Web3 education, AI automation tooling, publishing, and SaaS — all united by a single principle: build things people actually need, say what you mean, and own your distribution.

Each property under Buy One Media is designed to generate revenue independently while feeding into the others — books drive web traffic, tools serve book readers, communities amplify both. The goal isn't a single product. It's a system.
